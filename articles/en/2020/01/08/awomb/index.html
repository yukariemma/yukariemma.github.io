One of the must-go restaurant "AWOMB"

Their sushi is an art!

You get small piece of everything with all kinds of seasoning. Fresh fish with almond chips and a little bit of wasabi or fresh veggies with sesame sauce and a bit of cheese. How you eat and the combination are all up to you! By the time you finish, not only your stomach but your eyes are fulfilled.

They have stores in a few different places in Kyoto. They all have a different theme. Check AWOMB KYOTO and book in advance! You will never regret.
