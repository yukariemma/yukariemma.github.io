Copper from India

  • May 4, 2020
  • berlin

My best friend brought us copper cups from India.
It got black stain over some time. Yesterday I cleaned them with lemon and they look super shiny now :)

She and I have known each other since we were 7. There were some time that we have lost in touch but our paths crossed again quite naturally.

We spent our first year after university together in Tokyo. That was a quite fun time. Being 23 in a big city like Tokyo made a lot of things possible.

And I started wandering around the world, while she started building her career. But no matter where we were, we kept talking. Even though we started walking into very different directions, there was always a connection between us.

We don't talk everyday, we sometimes go over months without talking. But when we decide to call each other, it's not because we have some big life events to tell. We just talk as if we talked the day before.

A friend dear to my heart, to my life.

Genki? Maybe we should call :)
